# CGHS Rate List 2025 - Summary

## Implementation Date
Effective from October 13, 2025

## Room Rent Limits
- General Ward: Rs 1,500
- Semi-Private Ward: Rs 3,000
- Private Ward: Rs 4,500

## Rate Structure and Differentials
- Non-NABH/Non-NABL HCOs: 15% lower than NABH/NABL accredited hospitals
- Super Speciality Hospitals: 15% higher than NABH-accredited hospitals
- City Classification Adjustments:
  * Y (Tier II) cities: 10% lower than X (Tier I) cities
  * Z (Tier III) cities: 20% lower than Tier I cities
- Ward Entitlement Adjustments:
  * General ward: 5% decrease from semi-private rates
  * Private ward: 5% increase on semi-private rates

## Consultation and ICU Charges
- OPD/IPD Consultation: Rs 350 (uniform for NABH and Non-NABH)
- OPD Super Specialty/Psychiatry Consultation: Rs 700
- ICU Charges: Rs 5,400 (inclusive of room rent for all ward entitlements)

## Package Rate Definition
Package rates cover up to:
- 12 days for Specialized (Super Specialties) treatment
- 7 days for other Major Surgeries
- 3 days for Laparoscopic surgeries/elective Angioplasty/normal deliveries
- 1 day for day care/Minor (OPD) surgeries

## Key Validation Rules
1. Package rates are all-inclusive: accommodation, medicines, surgery, consultations, investigations, nursing care, ICU charges
2. Multiple procedures in same OT session:
   - Primary surgery: 100% of package rate
   - Second surgery: 50% of package rate
   - Third & subsequent: 25% of each package rate
3. Bilateral/identical procedures: Second procedure at 50%
4. Procedures within package period (same admission): 75% of applicable package rate

## Common Investigations (Semi-Private Ward, NABH, Tier I Cities)
- Complete Hemogram/CBC: Rs 300
- Blood Glucose Fasting/PP: Rs 40
- Serum Creatinine: Rs 100
- Liver Function Test (LFT): Rs 500
- Kidney Function Test (KFT): Rs 500
- Lipid Profile: Rs 490
- HbA1c: Rs 300
- Thyroid Function Test (TFT): Rs 450
- ECG: Rs 100
- X-Ray Chest PA View: Rs 200
- Ultrasound Abdomen: Rs 800
- CT Scan Head Plain: Rs 2,000
- MRI Brain Plain: Rs 4,500

Note: This is a summary. Full detailed rate list contains thousands of procedures and investigations.
